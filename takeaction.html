How to Help

5 ways to take action against fast fashion:

1. Buy from sustainable clothing brands

2. Donate old clothes to thrift stores instead of throwing them away

3. Bring unwearable clothes (ex clothes with big holes/rips) to textile recycling boxs in your city

4. Share clothes with your friends instead of always buying new ones

5. Purchase simple basic clothing that can we worn timelessly instead of trendy clothing that will soon go out of style.
